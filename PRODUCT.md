# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Dutch monument owners who want to make their homes more sustainable, but need clear guidance through a permit-heavy and often confusing process. They are often not experts in heritage rules, technical measures, or municipal procedures, and may already feel blocked or overwhelmed before they begin.

Secondary users are municipality workers and public-sector teams who may evaluate the product as an example worth adopting or adapting in their own systems.

## Product Purpose
This workspace explores and implements digital guidance tools that help monument owners prepare sustainability, maintenance, restoration, and permit-related decisions with more confidence and less friction.

Success means the product helps people move from uncertainty to action: understanding what applies to their building, what information they need, what tradeoffs matter, and how to prepare a stronger, calmer, more complete request or dossier.

## Brand Personality
Ontzorgend, behulpzaam, toegankelijk.

The voice should feel warm, practical, and human. It should guide people through complexity without sounding official, distant, or bureaucratic. The product should feel like capable support beside the user, especially when they are stuck in a landscape of permits, heritage constraints, and unclear next steps.

## Anti-references
This should not feel gemeentelijk, formal, or dependent on institutional language for authority.

It should not reduce the experience to numbers, compliance checklists, or cold procedural steps. It should not feel like a user is being left alone in a swamp of regulations, nor like they are being pushed through a rigid system that assumes prior knowledge.

## Design Principles
1. Lower stress before asking for precision.
2. Translate regulation into clear next steps.
3. Let guidance feel human, not institutional.
4. Build trust through clarity, not bureaucracy.
5. Keep the user moving, even when information is incomplete.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
No stricter accessibility requirement has been set for this workspace.

The product should still aim for clear language, readable contrast, calm interaction patterns, and interfaces that support users who may feel uncertain, overloaded, or unfamiliar with permit and heritage processes.
